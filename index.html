What is CampVivor?

An ORG Built for Discord

CampVivor is an Online Reality Game (ORG) — a fan-made, community-driven experience inspired by real-world survival competition shows. Every season, players compete through challenges, form alliances, and vote each other out until one sole survivor is crowned.

All the action unfolds live on Discord — strategy channels, tribal councils, immunity challenges, and confessionals. No downloads. No signup fees. Just pure social gameplay.
